Wish Sentences – Câu Ước
Unit 6 · Wishes & Regrets

▸ Wish + Past Simple (Hiện tại không có thật)

S + wish(es) + S + Vpast / were ...
→ I wish I knew the answer. (Thực tế: I don't know)
→ She wishes she were taller.
⚠ Dùng were (không phải was) cho tất cả ngôi trong văn viết trang trọng.

▸ Wish + Past Perfect (Quá khứ hối tiếc)

S + wish(ed) + S + had + Vp.p ...
→ I wish I had studied harder. (Thực tế: Tôi đã không học chăm)
→ They wish they hadn't missed the train.

▸ Wish + Would (Ao ước tương lai / chỉ trích)

S + wish(es) + S + would + Vbare
→ I wish it would stop raining.
→ I wish you wouldn't talk so loudly.
⚠ Không dùng wish ... would khi chủ ngữ hai vế giống nhau: ✗ I wish I would...

▸ So sánh nhanh: If only = Wish (mạnh hơn, cảm xúc hơn)

If only he had listened to me! ≈ I wish he had listened.
2
Conditional Type III – Điều Kiện Loại 3
Unit 7 · Hypothetical Past

▸ Công thức

If + S + had + Vp.p ..., S + would/could/might have + Vp.p
→ If she had left earlier, she would have caught the bus.
→ If they hadn't wasted time, they could have finished the project.

▸ Đảo ngữ (Inversion) – dạng trang trọng

Had + S + Vp.p ..., S + would have + Vp.p
→ Had she studied harder, she would have passed.

▸ Câu hỗn hợp (Mixed Conditional)

LoạiIf-clauseMain clauseÝ nghĩa
III → IIIf + had + p.pwould + VbareQuá khứ → ảnh hưởng hiện tại
II → IIIIf + Vpastwould have + p.pTính chất hiện tại → quá khứ
→ If I had taken that job, I would be rich now.
→ If she were more careful, she wouldn't have made that mistake.

▸ Unless / But for / Without trong điều kiện

But for your help, I would have failed. (= If it hadn't been for your help...)
Without his advice, she might have made a terrible decision.
3
Relative Clauses – Nâng Cao
Unit 8 · Defining & Non-defining

▸ Rút gọn mệnh đề quan hệ

LoạiCách rút gọnVí dụ
Active (chủ động)Bỏ who/which + đổi V → V-ingThe man talking to her is my teacher.
Passive (bị động)Bỏ who/which was/were + dùng Vp.pThe book written by Tolkien is famous.
Be + adj/nounBỏ who is / which isThe girl in red is my sister.

▸ Relative Adverbs: where / when / why

This is the school where I studied. (= at which)
That was the year when I graduated. (= in which)
I know the reason why she left. (= for which)

▸ What / Which (mệnh đề quan hệ bổ nghĩa cả câu)

She passed the exam, which surprised everyone. (= cả sự việc đó)
What he said shocked us all. (= điều mà)
⚠ What không bao giờ đứng sau danh từ. Which (non-defining) có thể thay thế cả mệnh đề.

▸ Lưu ý: Defining vs Non-defining

The student who studies hard will pass. (defining – không dấu phẩy)
My brother, who lives in Hanoi, is a doctor. (non-defining – có dấu phẩy)

▸ Reported Questions

S + asked + (O) + wh-word / if / whether + S + V (trật tự bình thường)
"Where do you live?" → She asked me where I lived.
"Are you happy?" → He wanted to know if/whether I was happy.

▸ Reported Commands & Requests

S + told/ordered/asked + O + (not) to + V
"Don't touch that!" → He told me not to touch that.
"Please help me." → She asked him to help her.

Modal Verbs + Perfect Infinitive
Unit 9–10 · Deduction, Regret & Criticism
Cấu trúcÝ nghĩaVí dụ
must have + p.p Khẳng định chắc chắn (quá khứ) She must have left early. (chắc chắn đã đi)
can't/couldn't have + p.p Phủ định chắc chắn He can't have stolen it.
may/might have + p.p Có thể đã... (không chắc) They might have missed the bus.
should have + p.p Đáng lẽ phải làm (tiếc nuối) You should have called me.
shouldn't have + p.p Đáng lẽ không nên làm She shouldn't have said that.
needn't have + p.p Không cần làm nhưng đã làm You needn't have cooked – we ordered food.
could have + p.p Có thể đã làm (nhưng không làm) He could have won if he'd tried harder.
💡 must have = strong positive deduction
can't have = strong negative deduction
should have = criticism / regret

Inversion – Đảo Ngữ
Unit 10 · Emphasis & Formal Style

▸ Đảo ngữ với trạng từ phủ định

Cụm từNghĩaVí dụ
NeverChưa bao giờNever have I seen such beauty.
Rarely / SeldomHiếm khiRarely does he complain.
Hardly / Scarcely ... whenVừa mới ... thìHardly had I arrived when it rained.
No sooner ... thanVừa ... đãNo sooner had she left than he called.
Not only ... but alsoKhông chỉ ... mà cònNot only did he lie, but he also stole.
Only when / if / afterChỉ khiOnly when he arrived did we start.
Under no circumstancesTrong mọi trường hợp đều khôngUnder no circumstances should you lie.

▸ Đảo ngữ với So/Neither/Nor

So + aux + S (đồng ý khẳng định)  |  Neither/Nor + aux + S (đồng ý phủ định)
"I love coffee." → "So do I."
"She can't swim." → "Neither can he."

▸ Đảo ngữ trong điều kiện (Conditional)

Were I you, I would apologise. (= If I were you)
Had she known, she would have helped. (= If she had known)
Should you need help, call me. (= If you should need)
